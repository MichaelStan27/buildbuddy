Build Buddy is a web-based application designed to assist users in easily and enjoyably assembling their dream PCs. Build Buddy comprises five main features:
System Builder - aids users in assembling PC components that are suitable for their needs.
Software Compatibility - enables users to check the compatibility between games and a PC's specifications.
Expert Consultation - serves as a platform for users to consult with experts regarding PC assembly.
Forum and Articles - provides users with a space to exchange ideas and gather information about PC assembly.
Build Buddy was developed by a trio from PPTI 10:
Michael Stanley, Fernando Clemente, Ethan Wang
Under the guidance of Professor Diana from Bina Nusantara University.
